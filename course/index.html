The traditional approach to AI learning is built on a fundamental lie.

The tech industry wants you to believe that to use AI effectively, you need to understand the underlying technology. They teach you about "prompt engineering frameworks," "model parameters," and "fine-tuning algorithms."

When developers teach AI, they're teaching you to think like a programmer. But marketers don't need to program AI — we need to direct AI.
